Local Cuisine

OBX is home to many unique and outstanding local restaurants, ranging from seafood, to authentic cuisine from cultures across the world. No matter what kind of food you like, you’ll find something amazing to eat close by.

Some notable spots on the island have even been recognized on the Food Network show, “Diners, Drive- ins, and Dives” by the host of the show, and legendary chef, Guy Fieri. Guy, along with many other locals and visitors highly recommend these restaurants...

Tortuga's Lie

Featured on “Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives”, Season 0 Episode 20 & Season 16 Episode 12

Located in Nags Head, slightly South of Kitty Hawk, Tortuga’s Lie is well known in OBX as a favorite.

With great food and a relaxing atmosphere, this restaurant is worth checking out when visiting the Outer Banks.

The Black Pelican Oceanfront Restaurant

Featured on “Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives”, Season 16 Episode 7

The Black Pelican is located in Kitty Hawk, placed right along the beach, serving outstanding seafood, as well as other cuisine that anyone would enjoy. Along side the food, they also have a huge selection of wines from around the globe.

I have been here myself in 2019, and the food and atmosphere are amazing. I would highly recommend checking this place out if you get the chance.

Coastal Cravings

Featured on “Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives”, Season 16 Episode 3

This is an extremely unique spot containing a drive-thru “Pot-to-Go” seafood boil. All you have to do is drive up, grab a massive pot filled with seafood and vegetables, and drive home to enjoy with family and friends.

Lesser Known Spots

While some people enjoy visiting to the most popular and well known restaurants and shops, local hidden gems are where its at. These not as well known local spots are just as good, and provide an amazing experience in terms of food, quality, and atmosphere.

Uncle Ike's Sandbar and Grill

Uncle Ike’s is one my favorite restaurants in OBX, offering some of the best food in Corolla. The location is surrounded by shops, and is a short walk from the beach. Its also very close to some of the Major Areas of Exploration mentioned above.

The service is outstanding, with friendly and funny employees, and is a must visit when staying in Corolla.

Timbuck II

This outdoor plaza is home to over 60 shops, restaurants, and activities, making it a great spot to spend a few hours when on vacation. Check out North Banks and Okinawa Sushi and Grill while you’re there!

Waterfront Shops

This is another outdoor shopping plaza, but this place is built right along the sound, allowing for a great view of inland North Carolina while you shop.

Duck’s Cottage is a small coffee shop/book store, serving great coffee and great service.

Untucked might seem like a normal clothing store, but besides the awesome clothes they sell and amazing deals year round, the owner and employees are some of the nicest people in OBX, and are happy to have a conversation.
