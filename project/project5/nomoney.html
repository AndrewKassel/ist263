Natural Beauty

With all of the shops, restaurants, and activities to do in OBX, sometimes it feels like you’re missing out on the natural beauty of the area. The Outer Banks has so many cool places to experience, without spending a cent.
If you’re looking for places like this, try these...

4x4 Section

Deflate your tires and drive up as far north as you can until you reach the 4x4 section in Corolla. You can take a drive right on the beach, passing by houses, lakes, ocean, and even wild horses that live in the area. If you go far enough north, you’ll reach huge sand dunes that look like you’re on a planet from Star Wars.
This is well worth the effort of making your car 4x4 ready, and is very memorable when spending time with family and friends. Good luck trying to drive your car on the beach somewhere else!

Nature Reserves

A few minutes walk from the 4x4 section, the Corolla Nature Reserve takes you on a walk through the woods where you will see deer, as well as other native animals. At the end of the walk, you’ll find yourself at a wooden lookout over the sound, looking out to the mainland.
This is a short walk, but well worth it since the end result is a great spot to hang out and get some great pictures and memories.

Wright Brothers National Memorial

The Wright Brothers achieved the first successful airplane flight in Kitty Hawk, with the area now known as the Wright Brothers National Museum. It contains a huge area full of statues, as well as grassy areas to walk around. If you like history or nature, this is a must visit spot, and knowing the first plane was flown off the ground you’re standing on is pretty awesome.

Jocky's Ridge State Park

This state park is, simply put, a massive sand dune that is connected to the sound. Similarly to the 4x4 section, this also makes you feel like you’re on another planet. There’s even hang gliding here, where you can get an immaculate view of the area and the water around you. This is located in Nags Head, south of Kitty Hawk, but is well worth the drive.

No matter where you are in the Outer Banks, there is something fun to do or experience. Everywhere you go you are only a few minutes from the beach, and a few minutes from great food and shops. When you visit, go explore the area and see what hidden gems you can find yourself. On your make, make sure to hit some of the places mentioned here, trust me, its worth it.
